## Problem Statement

In India, travelers, students, and working professionals face major challenges:

- Difficulty finding affordable and verified accommodations
- Lack of trusted shared housing options in new cities
- No personalized itinerary planning
- Fraudulent or unverified rental listings
- Poor integration between travel and long-term living needs

Existing platforms are fragmented and not optimized for Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities in Bharat.

---

## Proposed Solution

AwasAI is an AI-powered unified platform that:

- Recommends short-term stays (hotels, hostels, homestays)
- Provides verified shared flats for working professionals
- Generates personalized AI-based itineraries
- Suggests nearby attractions (Hidden Gems & Popular Spots)
- Ensures trust through verification badges and trust scores

---

## Core AI Components

### 1. Intelligent Recommendation Engine
- Uses user inputs (budget, duration, purpose of visit)
- Suggests optimized accommodation options
- Learns from user behavior over time

### 2. AI Itinerary Generator
- Generates day-by-day travel plans
- Optimizes based on time, distance, and preferences

### 3. Fraud Detection & Trust Scoring
- Detects suspicious listings using behavioral patterns
- Assigns trust scores to hosts and users
- Flags abnormal activity

### 4. Smart Matching for Shared Housing
- Matches professionals based on preferences
- Filters by gender, budget, and work location proximity
